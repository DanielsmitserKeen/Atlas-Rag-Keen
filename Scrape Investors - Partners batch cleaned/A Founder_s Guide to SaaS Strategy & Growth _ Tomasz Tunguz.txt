# A Founder's Guide to SaaS Strategy & Growth

Software as a Service has become the dominant business model for B2B software, but building a successful SaaS company requires mastering unique challenges around pricing, customer acquisition, retention, and unit economics. This guide synthesizes insights from analyzing hundreds of successful SaaS companies and their path to scale.
